DGA 1020

Vector Illustration




For this topic, we were required to create a vector art piece of a door or balcony.
The dimensions of the final product had to be sized A4 and it was to be created using Adobe Illustrator.
The task consisted of:


  • Explroing the island's colourful doors and balconies through visual research and documenting this through photography
  • Choosing a facade out of the images we took in order to recreate it as a vector illustration
  • Sketching and planing in order to have a clear idea of how we are going to translate the image into vector shapes
  • And lastly, creating the illustration in Illustrator
